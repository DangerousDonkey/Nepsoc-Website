Welcome To Warwick Nepsoc

Who are we?

We are The University of Warwick's Nepalese Society where you can meet the Nepali students from all years, alumni and others interested in our culture.

What do we do?

We regularly host a variety of fun and engaging events, giving you the chance to meet and socialize with students from all backgrounds — not just the Nepali community. From our famous Momo Nights and lively bar crawls to games nights, arts and crafts sessions, and cultural celebrations, there’s something for everyone.

Beyond social events, we also organise career-focused workshops and networking sessions, helping our members gain valuable insights into different industries, develop professional skills, and explore potential career paths. Whether you’re here to make friends, celebrate Nepali culture, or prepare for your future, there’s a place for you in Warwick NepSoc.
